About Brain Consulting & Development

Brain Consulting & Development delivers expert level knowledge of the bicycle industry and supply chain, working with suppliers spanning the globe, to optimize quality, manage costs, and reduce time to market. Deeply rooted in the bicycle industry, Brain can also create cross-functional teams of product managers, industrial designers, engineers as well as graphic designers. With the ability to oversee the entire product development process, from conception to sourcing, from manufacturing to delivery, our goal is to ensure quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction every step of the way.
